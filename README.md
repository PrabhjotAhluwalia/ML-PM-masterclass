# ML-PM Masterclass

This repository contains book-length PDF manuscripts for product managers rebuilding or deepening technical fluency in AI, ML, deep learning, and LLM systems.

**Volume 1: The Foundational Mathematics Primer for Advanced AI, ML, and LLMs — An Exhaustive PM Masterclass**

**Volume 2: AI, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and LLMs — An Exhaustive PM Masterclass**

The manuscripts are written for senior AI product managers who need both technical depth and production operating judgment. Volume 1 builds the mathematical foundation across linear algebra, probability and statistics, calculus, optimization, information theory, discrete mathematics, and time-series math. Volume 2 extends that foundation into classical machine learning, deep learning, LLMs, reinforcement learning, game AI, ML for trading, AI product management, enterprise agentic AI, MLOps, data flywheels, serving infrastructure, AI UX, regulatory governance, commercialization strategy, and failure-mode postmortems.

## Contents

- `volume-1-foundational-mathematics-primer.pdf` — the foundational mathematics primer for advanced AI, ML, and LLMs, with fintech worked examples, PM relevance boxes, NumPy snippets, and appendices.
- `volume-2-ai-ml-dl-llms-pm-masterclass-expanded.pdf` — the expanded AI, ML, deep learning, and LLM manuscript, with a clickable table of contents, worked examples, PM relevance boxes, runnable Python snippets, and appendices.

## Intended use

Use this as a technical refresher, interview preparation reference, and operating manual for building AI products across fintech, consumer, marketplace, and B2B SaaS contexts.
